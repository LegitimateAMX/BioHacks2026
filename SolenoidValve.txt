Requires: Arduino UNO, breadboard, IRFZ44N MOSFET, power source, solenoid, 1N4007 diode, 10kΩ resistor, 220Ω resistor

- Connect Arduino GND pin to GND rail, and the 19V source's GND to establish shared electrical reference.
- Place the MOSFET on the breadboard; connect its left pin (Gate) to Arduino Pin 9 via the 220Ω resistor.
- Bridge the 10kΩ resistor from the Gate directly to Ground to prevent floating voltage errors.
- Connect the right pin (Source) to Ground, leaving the middle pin (Drain) open for the solenoid.
- Wire the positive terminal of the solenoid directly to the positive output of your 19V power source.
- Connect the negative terminal of the solenoid to the middle pin (Drain) of your IRFZ44N MOSFET.
- Place the 1N4007 flyback diode across the solenoid's terminals with the striped end (cathode) facing the 19V positive connection.
